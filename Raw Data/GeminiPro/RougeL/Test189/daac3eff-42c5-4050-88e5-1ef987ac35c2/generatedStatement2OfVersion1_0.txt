

**Test Cases:**

**Normal Case:**

Input: "Hello World"
Output: "World Hello"

**Corner Cases:**

**Empty String:**

Input: ""
Output: ""

**Single Word:**

Input: "Hello"
Output: "Hello"

**Multiple Spaces:**

Input: "Hello    World"
Output: "World Hello"

**Leading and Trailing Spaces:**

Input: "  Hello World  "
Output: "World Hello"

**Special Characters:**

Input: "Hello! World?"
Output: "World? Hello!"
