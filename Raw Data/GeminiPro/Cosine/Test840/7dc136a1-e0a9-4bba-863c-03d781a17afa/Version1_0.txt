**Explanation of Results and Integer Division:**

* **Integer Division:** In Java, when you divide two integers (like `-15 / 8`), the result is also an integer.  The fractional part is truncated (discarded). So, `-15 / 8` becomes `-1`.

* **Expression c:**  The original calculation `20 + -3 * 5 / 8` proceeds as follows:
   1. `-3 * 5 = -15`
   2. `-15 / 8 = -1` (integer division)
   3. `20 + -1 = 18`

This forces the division to be performed with floating-point precision. You can then round to 19 if that's required by the problem.
