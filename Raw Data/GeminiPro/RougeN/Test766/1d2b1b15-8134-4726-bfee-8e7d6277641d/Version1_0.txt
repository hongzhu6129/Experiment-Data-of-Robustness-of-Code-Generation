Explanation:

1. **Declare Variables:** We declare two integer variables, `num1` and `num2`, and initialize them with the values 74 and 36, respectively.  We also declare a variable `sum` to store the result of the addition.

2. **Calculate Sum:**  The line `int sum = num1 + num2;` calculates the sum of `num1` and `num2` and stores the result in the `sum` variable.

3. **Print Output:** `System.out.println(...)` prints the result to the console. We use string concatenation (`+`) to create a clear output message.
