


**Test Cases:**

Here are some test cases, including corner and edge cases:

1. **Regular Positive Numbers:**
   - Input: `5 10 15`
   - Expected Output: `10.0`

2. **Zeroes:**
   - Input: `0 0 0`
   - Expected Output: `0.0`

3. **Negative Numbers:**
   - Input: `-5 -10 -15`
   - Expected Output: `-10.0`

4. **Mixed Positive and Negative:**
   - Input: `-5 10 -5`
   - Expected Output: `0.0`

5. **Large Numbers:**
   - Input: `1000000 2000000 3000000`
   - Expected Output: `2000000.0`

6. **Decimal Numbers:**
   - Input: `2.5 5.5 7.5`
   - Expected Output: `5.166666666666667`

7. **Corner Case - Very large/small numbers (to test potential overflow/underflow if integers were used):**
   - Input: `Double.MAX_VALUE Double.MIN_VALUE 0`  (Java's max and min double values)
   - Expected Output: `8.988465674311579E307`  (Expect a very large positive number)


8. **Invalid Input:**
   - Input: `a b c`
   - Expected Output: `"Invalid input. Please enter numbers only."`

9. **Partial Invalid Input:**
   - Input: `1 2 a`
   - Expected Output: `"Invalid input. Please enter numbers only."`

10. **Numbers with leading/trailing spaces (to ensure the scanner handles whitespace correctly):**
   - Input: `  10   20 30   `
   - Expected Output: `20.0`
