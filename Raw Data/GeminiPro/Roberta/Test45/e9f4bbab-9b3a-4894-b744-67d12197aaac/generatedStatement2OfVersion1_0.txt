

**Test cases including corner cases:**

* **Empty string:** ""
* **Single character:** "a"
* **Single odd-length word:** "This is a test with a single odd-length word"
* **Multiple odd-length words:** "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit"
* **Odd-length word at the beginning:** "12345 67890 123456789"
* **Odd-length word at the end:** "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Mauris ut tincidunt nunc, eget consectetur nulla dignissim et."
* **String with only even-length words:** "12345 67890"
* **String with only odd-length words:** "a b c d e f"
* **String with a mix of even-length and odd-length words:** "This is a test"
