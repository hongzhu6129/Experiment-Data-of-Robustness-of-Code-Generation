

**Test cases:**

* **Corner cases:**
    * Two chords with the same name (e.g., "C" and "c")
    * Two chords with the same name but different casing (e.g., "C" and "C")
    * Two enharmonic chords with different names (e.g., "C#" and "Db")

* **General cases:**
    * Two chords with different names (e.g., "C" and "D")
    * Two chords with different qualities (e.g., "C" and "Cmaj")
    * Two chords with different extensions (e.g., "C" and "C7")
    * Two chords with different inversions (e.g., "C" and "C/E")
    * Two chords with different bass notes (e.g., "C" and "C/G")
