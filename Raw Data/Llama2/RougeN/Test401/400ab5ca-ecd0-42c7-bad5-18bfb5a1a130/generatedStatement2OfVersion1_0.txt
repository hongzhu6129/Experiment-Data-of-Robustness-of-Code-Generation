

**Test Cases:**

* **Normal case:**
    * Input: "Hello World!"
    * Output: "Helo Wrd!"

* **Empty string:**
    * Input: ""
    * Output: ""

* **String with all duplicate characters:**
    * Input: "aaaaaa"
    * Output: "a"

* **String with special characters:**
    * Input: "Hello, World!"
    * Output: "Hel, Wor!"

* **String with numbers:**
    * Input: "1234567890"
    * Output: "1234567890"

* **String with spaces:**
    * Input: "Hello World  "
    * Output: "Helo Wrd "
