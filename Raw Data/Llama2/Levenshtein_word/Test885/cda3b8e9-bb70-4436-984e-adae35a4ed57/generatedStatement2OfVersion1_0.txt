

**Test Cases:**

Here are some test cases, including corner and edge cases:

1. **General Case:**
   - Input: 5, 10, 15
   - Expected Output: 10.0

2. **Zeroes:**
   - Input: 0, 0, 0
   - Expected Output: 0.0

3. **Negative Numbers:**
   - Input: -5, -10, -15
   - Expected Output: -10.0

4. **Mixed Positive and Negative:**
   - Input: -5, 10, 5
   - Expected Output: 3.3333333333333335  (or similar due to floating-point representation)

5. **Large Numbers:**
   - Input: 1000000, 2000000, 3000000
   - Expected Output: 2000000.0

6. **Small Numbers (close to zero):**
   - Input: 0.001, 0.002, 0.003
   - Expected Output: 0.002

7. **Decimal Numbers:**
    - Input: 2.5, 5.7, 8.2
    - Expected Output: 5.466666666666667 (or similar)
