

**Test Cases:**

**Corner Cases:**

* **Empty string:** ""
* **String with only one character:** "a"
* **String with two characters that are the same:** "aa"
* **String with two characters that are different:** "ab"

**Normal Cases:**

* **String with an odd length palindrome:** "abba"
* **String with an even length palindrome:** "racecar"
* **String with multiple palindromes:** "abbaabba"
* **String with no palindromes:** "abcdefghij"
